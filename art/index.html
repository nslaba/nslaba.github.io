thoughts

The point of art for me has always been to play around and try to find something more true. The thoughts we possess in language are already bound to certain restrictions — we tell ourselves stories of our perceived reality, yet a deeper truth exists in something more spontaneous and intuitive. Art can serve as a format to explore those feelings and to liberate oneself from oneself at times.

Art shouldn't be treated merely as a product. It's something from which we should learn.
