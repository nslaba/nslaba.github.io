I make conceptual paintings in layered acrylic and acrylic ink. Human figures show up a lot — because people genuinely puzzle me, the decisions we make, the brief specific lives we lead inside these bodies. Based in Squamish, BC. Read more →

selected works

At that scale, your whole body is in it — I'd often find myself dancing while I worked. It's also about as far from elitist as art gets. You walk past it. No ticket.

I think the rawest forms of creativity exist in unpolished works. Simple momentary expressions.

An experiment in accessibility — printing my art on everyday objects so it can live in your pocket, your morning routine, wherever.
