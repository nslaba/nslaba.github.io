Commissions

A painting made
for you

I take on portraits, abstract work, and murals. It starts with a conversation — tell me what you have in mind.

What I take on
Portraits

Figurative work — people, moments, faces. I'm more interested in capturing something true than something flattering.

Abstract

Conceptual and layered work in acrylic and acrylic ink. Works best when you come with a feeling or an idea rather than a specific image — I'll figure out the rest.

Murals

Large-scale work for interior or exterior spaces. Tell me about the wall, the context, and what you want to say with it. Quoted by project.
